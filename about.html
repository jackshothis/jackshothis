Hi, I'm Jack — and this is Jack Shot This.

I'm a photographer and videographer based in Vancouver, BC, working across a wide range of styles and projects. Most of my work is in real estate, but I don't limit myself to one genre. Over the past 5+ years, I've shot everything from portraits and landscapes to product photography, events, architecture, and sports — whatever catches my eye or tells a good story.

My work is all about adaptability. I tailor each shoot to the project and the people involved, whether that means capturing a quiet moment, a fast-paced event, or a clean, polished look for e-commerce. My editing style shifts depending on the vibe, but lately I've been drawn to a more cinematic approach — simple, intentional, and honest. Less is more.

I love being outdoors, exploring new techniques, and constantly learning. Whether I'm flying a drone, using a 360° camera, or working with just natural light, my goal is always the same: to capture something that feels real — something that matters later.

When you work with JST, you're not just hiring someone to click a shutter. You're collaborating with someone who cares about the bigger picture — your story, your message, your moment.

Let's shoot something worth remembering!
